One sunny weekend, Maya and Tom visited their grandma's cozy cottage by the countryside. Exploring the kitchen, they stumbled upon a recipe book with Grandma's favorite cookie recipes. Intrigued by the delicious possibilities, Grandma decided to teach them about fractions.

Excited to learn, Maya and Tom eagerly measured and mixed ingredients under Grandma's guidance, turning fractions into tasty treats. By the end of the day, they not only enjoyed warm, freshly baked cookies but also discovered the sweet simplicity of math in the kitchen.
